Dylan Sprouse

Website Designer & Technical Systems Builder

My Approach

I design and build structured, performance-focused websites using clean, custom-written code. I do not rely on bloated page builders or pre-built templates unless specifically requested.

Every project begins with understanding the system — how the business operates, how users make decisions, and how structure influences action.

How I Think

I approach design analytically. A website is not decoration — it is a system.

Structure drives clarity. Clarity drives trust. Trust drives action.

I prioritize logic, flow, and controlled architecture over visual noise.

Systems I’ve Built

Before founding DS Digital Designs, I built internal tools and structured systems that improved efficiency and understanding within professional environments.

One example includes developing an escrow calculator tool used by agents to better understand and explain escrow shortages. The tool simplified complex calculations and improved clarity across the team.

I’ve also built structured note-taking systems, internal documentation frameworks, and operational tools designed to reduce confusion and increase consistency.

I build with long-term scalability in mind — not short-term shortcuts.

Why Custom Code

Writing code directly provides full control over structure, performance, and scalability.

Pre-built templates often introduce unnecessary complexity and limitations. By building from the ground up, I maintain clarity and flexibility in every system.
